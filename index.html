Misinformation never stops. So neither do we.

Modern problems require modern solutions.

There are massive server farms dedicated to churning out fake news. Why are we relying on a handful of human fact-checkers to counter them?

Checks statements

Just highlight a statement and run a fact check on it. No sifting through google search, and no trying to figure out whether a source is trustworthy or not -- we do that for you too.

Social media detector

No more misinformation and spam on your Facebook and Twitter feeds. We find the sponsor of all sponsored posts and tell you, as well as fact-checking the posts themselves.

Adblocker

Traditional adblockers are incapable of stopping political ads. But with us, you'll never get a politcal pop-up again.

Available for all

Fact checking should be accessible for everyone, not hidden behind a paywall, or excluded from people who don't speak English.

Why it matters

Right now, there are wars on 4 out of our 6 inhabited continents, the American government is in turmoil, and we're gearing up for yet another election (which has already produced its fair share of misinformation). Agendas are becoming more heated, and politicians more desperate. And that means we're seeing a fake news epidemic like never before, without the proper tools to combat it.

Frequently asked questions

Some questions you might have.

Is factfully up-to-date?

Yes. Our live feed of fact-checks sourced from throughout the web allows us to always remain accurate.

Is factfully 100% accurate?

Unfortunately, not yet. We're only as reliable as our sources. There are many thousands of them, but if there's a failure which results in a lot of them systematically misreporting something, we will probably misreport it too.

How does factfully deal with half-truths?

Our rating system recognizes that some things may occupy a grey area between true and false. Our ratings reflect this, with some ratings such as 'misleading', 'out of context', etc.

Does factfully collect my data?

Short answer: no, we collect nothing. But to put yourself at ease, check out our privacy policy.

Is factfully an adblocker or a fact-checker?

We're both. Our software blocks ads (with a special focus on political ads) while also fact-checking statements.
